Stronghold Spawn Simulator

This app can be used to simulate Stronghold spawns using the following rules:

- A Stronghold spawn is determined by the % of WarZone Points a faction owns. A higher % results in a stronger SH spawn.
- A Stronghold will determine sector ownership: own the SH to control the sector.
- Evil King strongholds contain a large amount of wall defences.
